🚀 Solar System Explorer

Embark on an interactive journey through space! Explore realistic orbital mechanics, learn about celestial bodies, and customize your own solar system simulation.

What You'll Experience

  • Navigate through 3D space with intuitive controls
  • Realistic orbital mechanics and physics
  • Educational overlays about planets and moons
  • Interactive time controls to speed up orbits
  • Detailed information about each celestial body
  • Customizable solar system with multiple planets

🪐 Customize Your Solar System

Choose which celestial bodies to include in your exploration. Click to add or remove objects for a clearer view.

🎮 How to Play

Mouse Controls: Click and drag to pan around space, scroll to zoom in/out

Navigation: Use focus buttons to quickly jump to planets

Time Control: Adjust simulation speed to see orbital patterns

Learning: Click on planets to discover fascinating facts

🔬 Educational Features

Orbital Mechanics: See how gravity shapes planetary paths

Scale & Distance: Understand the vastness of space

Physics Concepts: Learn about gravitational forces and Kepler's laws

Real Data: All measurements based on actual astronomical data

🌟 Customization Tips

Focus Mode: Remove distant planets to study inner solar system

Comparison: Add multiple planets to compare sizes and orbits

Performance: Fewer objects = smoother animation

Learning: Start simple, then add complexity gradually
