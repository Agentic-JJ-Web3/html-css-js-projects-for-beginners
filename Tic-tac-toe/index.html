Tic-Tac-Toe

The Classic Game, Reimagined

Simple. Fun. Timeless.

Tic-Tac-Toe is the perfect game for two players! Take turns placing X's and O's on a 3×3 grid. The first player to get three in a row (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally) wins!

How to Play: Player 1 (X) goes first. Click any empty square to place your mark. The first to get three in a row wins! If all squares are filled with no winner, it's a draw.
